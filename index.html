  • Google Finance — the Average of Wall Street analysts' 12-month price targets shown on google.com/finance. Published mainly for US-listed stocks; used first where available.
  • Yahoo Finance — the mean analyst 12-month price target (targetMeanPrice) across the analysts covering the stock. Covers most international names.
  • TradingView — the 1-year analyst consensus target (price target). Used only where the two sources above have none (e.g. China A-shares).

Brokerage note: each figure is an aggregated consensus of price targets from multiple sell-side brokerages / research houses covering the stock. Google Finance, Yahoo Finance and TradingView publish only the aggregate (and, where shown, the number of contributing analysts) — they do not disclose the individual brokerages behind each target, so specific broker names cannot be attributed here. ETFs and newly listed DRs typically have no analyst coverage and therefore no target. Targets are analysts' opinions, not guarantees, and change frequently.

Exchange rates used in the calculation

Target Price (THB) = local-currency analyst target × exchange rate to THB ÷ conversion ratio. These rates fluctuate continuously and may differ from the rates actually available for currency conversion, from the DR issuer's conversion / settlement rate, and from interbank or retail rates at any given moment. They are indicative only and are not a quote or an offer to transact at these levels.
